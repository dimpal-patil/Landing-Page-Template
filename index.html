Chocolate Fudge

Description

This foolproof chocolate fudge delivers an incredibly rich, dense, and velvety texture with minimal effort. By combining high-quality chocolate with sweet condensed milk, you achieve a professional-grade confection that melts beautifully on the tongue without any complicated candy thermometers.

It is the perfect quick-fix dessert for holiday gifting, party platters, or satisfying a sudden sweet craving. The recipe serves as an excellent blank canvas, allowing you to easily customize the final flavor profile with your favorite chopped nuts, crushed cookies, or aromatic extracts.
